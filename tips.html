Help — Annika commands

The exact phrasings Annika recognizes. Skim the Quick Start, then dig in as needed.

Think of Annika as your personal macro tracker — be specific when you can, general when you want.

The commands below give you exact control. Plain English works too — Annika will do her best to interpret it — but commands skip the interpretation and go straight to the right action. This page covers everything Annika does.

Talking to Annika with Siri

Save the number in your contacts. Add +1-406-510-5510 as a contact named Annika.
Send a message by voice. “Siri, text Annika protein shake and two oat pancakes” — Siri dictates, confirms, and sends. Annika's reply comes back as a normal text.

Logging food

Plain text. Describe what you ate; Annika estimates the macros.
worksyogurt and blueberries
With a quantity. A leading number multiplies the serving.
works2 oatmeal
won't workoatmeal x2
With your own macros. Food name first, then the calorie number is required; carbs, protein, fat, fiber, and sodium are optional.
worksoatmeal 150 cals 27g carbs 5g protein
won't work150 calories oatmeal
Photo of a nutrition label. Send the photo. On SMS, Annika asks for serving size before logging.
Meal periods (Morning, Afternoon, Evening) are assigned automatically by time of day. Don't prefix with breakfast: or lunch: — that becomes part of the food name.

For SMS users

Don't text STOP, START, HELP, CANCEL, END, QUIT, or UNSUBSCRIBE — your carrier intercepts these as opt-out keywords and Annika won't see them. To cancel a back-and-forth, use never mind instead.
